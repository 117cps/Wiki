# Helmet of Heroism

![heroism](https://vwiki.valorserver.com/api/item/picture/helmet%20of%20heroism)

Effect on Self: Berserk for 6 seconds

Effect on Self: Invisible for 3 seconds

MP Cost: 115

Cooldown: 7 seconds

    +80 Maximum MP

Fame Bonus: 6%
