# Heatblast Trap

![heatblast](https://vwiki.valorserver.com/api/item/picture/heatblast%20trap)

<i>A trap that sends a blast of heat to the victim and burns their defenses right off.</i>

Trap: 750 HP within 8 sqrs for 6 seconds

MP Cost: 100

    +60 Maximum HP
    
    +6 Defense
    
    +6 Attack

Fame Bonus: 6%
