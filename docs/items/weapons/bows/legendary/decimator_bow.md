# Decimator Bow

![Decimator Bow](https://vwiki.valorserver.com/api/item/picture/decimator%20bow)

<i> Dark spirits influence the prey of this bow. In the wrong hands this weapon is fatal.</i>

Damage: 105-135

Shots: 6

Rate of Fire: 115%

Range 6.9 

Shots hit multiple targets

Fame Bonus: 8%
