# Guardian Dagger

![Guardian Dagger](https://vwiki.valorserver.com/api/item/picture/guardian%20dagger)

**Drops from Bridge Sentinel in the Shatters**

Shots: 4

Rate of Fire: 100%

Range: 6.6

Ignores defense of target

5% to inflict Stunned for 2 seconds
