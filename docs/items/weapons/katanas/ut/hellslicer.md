# Hellslicer

![Hellslicer](https://vwiki.valorserver.com/api/item/picture/hellslicer)

Damage: 160-210

Shots: 1

Rate of Fire: 100% 

Range: 4.3

Shots hit multiple targets

Ignores defense of target

Shots pass through obstacles
