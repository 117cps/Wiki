# Impacable Ram

![onlyfblance](https://vwiki.valorserver.com/api/item/picture/implacable%20ram)

Damage: 120-200

Shots: 4

Rate of Fire: 80%

Range: 3.3

Ignores defense of target

6% to inflict Stunned for 2.5s

    +75 Maximum HP

Fame Bonus: 6%
