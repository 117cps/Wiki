# Glaive of Victory

![GlaiveVic](https://vwiki.valorserver.com/api/item/picture/glaive%20of%20victory)

Damage: 142-190

Shots: 2

Rate of Fire: 100% 

Range: 4.5

Shots hit multiple targets

    +20 Restoration
