# Virulent Lance

![VLance](https://vwiki.valorserver.com/api/item/picture/virulent%20lance)

Damage: 95-150

Shots: 3

Rate of Fire: 110%

Range: 6

Shots hit multiple targets

Ignores defense of target

    +6 Speed

Fame Bonus: 6%
